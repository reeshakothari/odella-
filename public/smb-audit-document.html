Recommended Actions

Priority order. Not everything at once. Focus wins.
Priority 01 — Immediate
Fix the Foundation First
Address all CRITICAL items before anything else. A broken foundation makes every other investment worthless. Typically: name conflict resolution, logo redesign, website creation, Google Business setup.
Priority 02 — Month 1
Build the Digital Presence
Get the website live, SEO basics in place, social profiles completed and consistent. This is what converts strangers into enquiries.
Priority 03 — Month 2
Automate the Operations
Booking system, payment automation, CRM setup. Stop losing revenue to inefficiency. Every hour saved here is an hour that goes into growth.
Priority 04 — Month 3+
Layer in AI
Once the infrastructure exists, AI tools compound it. Chatbots, content automation, analytics dashboards — these only work when there is something real to automate on top of.
